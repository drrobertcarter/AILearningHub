Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

Account data. If you create an account, we store your email address, display name, and learning data (lesson progress, quiz results, certificates, and productivity metrics) in our database, hosted by Supabase. This data exists so your progress follows you across devices.

Local data. Chat history, assistant settings, and productivity metrics are also stored in your browser's local storage on your device.

API keys. If you provide an Anthropic API key for the assistant or prompt-execution features, it is stored only in your browser's local storage and sent only to Anthropic's API. It never touches our servers.

Newsletter. If you subscribe, we store your email address to send occasional updates. Unsubscribe anytime by replying to any issue.

Analytics. We log anonymous page views (page path, referring site, browser type). No advertising trackers, no third-party analytics scripts, no sale of data — ever.

Your rights

You can export your learning data anytime from the app. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at drrobertcarter@gmail.com and we will remove it.
